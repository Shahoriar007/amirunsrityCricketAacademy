Appreciation Reception

from Ex- BCB President on being champion on the BCB Academy Cup 2019

About the Event.

The BCB Academy Cup 2019, organized by the Bangladesh Cricket Board, was an elite tournament featuring 72 BCB-approved academies out of over 400 in the country. Amirun Srity Cricket Academy, one of the select few approved academies, participated in this prestigious event and brought the champions trophy to home.

Date
2019
Amirun Srity Cricket Academy's triumph in the BCB Academy Cup 2019 stands as a testament to our dedication, skill, and teamwork. Emerging as the undefeated champions among 72 of Bangladesh's top cricket academies is a remarkable feat that has catapulted our academy into the national spotlight. This victory not only showcases the exceptional talent of our players but also highlights the world-class coaching and training methods employed by our academy. We are immensely proud of this achievement and see it as a stepping stone towards nurturing future cricket stars for Bangladesh.
